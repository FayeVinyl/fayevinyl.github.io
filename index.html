About Me

I've spent the last seventeen years working as a detail-oriented, quality-focused sterile processing technician and am now ready to transfer my skills into the tech world.

  • Name: Timmie Faye DeWan
  • Age: 37
  • Interests: Web development, sewing, reading, knitting, learning, cooking, baking, cuddling with animals, and riding my motorcycle

My grandparents were the first to get a computer when I was a child, they let me use it when I was at their house one night and I was immediately hooked. I loved talking to people from around the world, being able to search for any topic and read about my interests. Later, like many others of my generation, I would spend hours customizing my myspace page with HTML and CSS. I started creating basic websites on Geocities, took a few classes in high school on java and web design. Since then I have taken various online courses over the years. I recently completed Meta's Front-End web developer class on Coursera and learned so much but my favorite resource has been freeCodeCamp.
